#### Criteria to select an insurance company
- Incurred Claims Ratio : The proportion of claims paid by an insurance company compared to the total premiums collected in a given financial year. And healthy claim ratio for a insurance company is around 0.8. Which means if the company collected RS 100/- as a insurance amount and from that RS 80/- used as a claim amount.
- Number of complaint in 10000 claims : How many complaint that insurance company got in 10000 claim. Average 22 is the max threshold that we can consider, which means any insurance has less than equal to 22 we can consider that insurance facility. 
#### Senior citizen health insurance policy what facility must need to have: 
##### Three below features must not have in your insurance.
- Room Rent Limit : Room rent limit decide how much claim amount the insurance company paid, for example , if you admitted to a hospital and where the room rent is RS 10000/- per day, but you had taken the policy which has room rent limit RS 5000/- in in that case only 50% of your total amount will be seattled by the insurance company. 
- Copay : How much percentage you have to pay from your the insurance holder pocket. 
- Disease wise sub-limit : Which means for any specific disease the insurance company limit , how much of the total sum assured value that you can claim, for example if you has RS 1500000/- sum assured then for diabetes patient you can claim only RS 500000 /- amount , this is called Disease wise sum-limit.
##### Must have features
- Renewal benefit or no claim benefit : For example , if you do not claim any in any particular year, if you do not claim anything then the bonus amount will be reward to the next financial year.
- Additional Cover 
- Restoration benefit : if you exhaust whole amount in a financial year , then one time restore will be allowed, for some agency multiple restore allowed.
- Less waiting period for pre-Existing disease 
- Less waiting period for special / slow growing diseases.
- Domiciliary Hospitalization : If your treatment is happing from the home, then that exapenses also can be claimed.
- Pre & Post Hospitalization : For best policies 90 days of pre hospitalization and max 180 days of post hospitalization can be offered.
- Coverage for AYUAH Treatment
